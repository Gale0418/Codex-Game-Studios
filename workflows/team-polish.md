# Team Polish

## Purpose

- Focus on polish, hardening, and final quality cleanup.

## Trigger

- Use when the feature works but needs refinement.

## Primary lane

- `performance-analyst`
- `ui-ux`
- `qa-regression`

## Inputs

- Working feature
- Cleanup targets
- Risk notes
- Regression concerns

## Steps

1. Trim the rough edges without reopening the main design.
2. Focus on visible quality, stability, and consistency.
3. Keep the polish pass small enough to verify.
4. Report what still feels unfinished.

## Outputs

- Cleanup list
- Risk notes
- Verification notes

## Exit criteria

- The feature is noticeably cleaner without adding new risk.

## Template

- `templates/team-polish-plan.md`
