# Team Audio

## Purpose

- Focus on sound design, feedback, and mix behavior.

## Trigger

- Use when audio cues or feedback loops change.

## Primary lane

- `audio-director`
- `sound-designer`
- `implementation`

## Inputs

- Audio cues
- Feedback loops
- Trigger points
- Mixing constraints

## Steps

1. Identify the events that need sound or feedback.
2. Separate design intent from implementation detail.
3. Keep the mix and timing readable.
4. Verify the cues in context.

## Outputs

- Audio decisions
- Integration notes
- Verification notes

## Exit criteria

- The cue behavior is implemented and audibly checked.

- `templates/team-audio-plan.md`
