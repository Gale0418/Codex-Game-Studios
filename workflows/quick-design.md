# Quick Design

## Purpose

- Produce a fast design direction for a small or early-stage idea.

## Trigger

- Use when the design needs a quick directional pass.

## Primary lane

- `game-designer`
- `creative-director`
- `systems-designer`

## Inputs

- Idea
- Constraints
- Known risks

## Steps

1. State the most likely direction.
2. Call out the biggest constraint.
3. Note the next design step.
4. Keep the output short.

## Outputs

- Direction
- Constraints
- Next step

## Exit criteria

- The user has a clear next design move.

- `templates/quick-design.md`
