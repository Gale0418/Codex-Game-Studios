# Milestone Review

## Purpose

- Decide whether a milestone can ship or must be cut back.

## Trigger

- Use at the end of a larger phase.

## Primary lane

- `producer`
- `qa-lead`
- `technical-director`

## Inputs

- Milestone scope
- Delivery evidence
- Open risks
- Test coverage

## Steps

1. Compare the milestone outcome against the plan.
2. Separate wins from remaining gaps.
3. Decide whether to ship, cut back, or continue.
4. Record the next checkpoint.

## Outputs

- Status
- Wins
- Gaps

## Exit criteria

- The milestone decision is unambiguous.
