# Design Review

## Purpose

- Review game design for clarity, risk, and player impact.

## Trigger

- Use before implementation when a design decision is still soft.

## Primary lane

- `game-designer`
- `systems-designer`
- `creative-director`

## Inputs

- Design notes
- Player goals
- Constraints
- Comparable patterns

## Steps

1. Restate the design problem in plain terms.
2. Compare the likely player impact of each option.
3. Call out risky assumptions and missing rules.
4. Recommend the cleanest direction for implementation.

## Outputs

- Strengths
- Concerns
- Recommendations

## Exit criteria

- The design has a clear direction and known risks.

- `templates/design-review-report.md`
