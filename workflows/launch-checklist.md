# Launch Checklist

## Purpose

- Confirm launch readiness before release.

## Trigger

- Use just before shipping or publishing.

## Primary lane

- `release-manager`
- `qa-lead`
- `producer`

## Inputs

- Final diff
- QA evidence
- Known blockers
- Release notes draft

## Steps

1. Confirm the ship list and open blockers.
2. Check the final verification evidence.
3. Ensure the release path and rollback notes are clear.
4. Hand off the launch decision.

## Outputs

- Checklist
- Blockers
- Notes

## Exit criteria

- The release can be approved or explicitly blocked.
