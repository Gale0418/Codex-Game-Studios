# Team Level

## Purpose

- Focus on level layout, pacing, and spatial flow.

## Trigger

- Use when level structure or encounter flow changes.

## Primary lane

- `level-designer`
- `game-designer`
- `implementation`

## Inputs

- Layout goals
- Encounter flow
- Spatial constraints
- Progression notes

## Steps

1. Map the intended pacing and spatial rhythm.
2. Identify the choke points and empty spaces.
3. Keep layout changes aligned with the rules and objectives.
4. Verify the flow in play.

## Outputs

- Layout notes
- Pacing notes
- Implementation notes

## Exit criteria

- The level or encounter flow makes sense in play and in layout.

## Template

- `templates/team-level-plan.md`
