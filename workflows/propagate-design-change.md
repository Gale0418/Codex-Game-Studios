# Propagate Design Change

## Purpose

- Push a design change through the affected systems and docs.

## Trigger

- Use when a design decision affects more than one area.

## Primary lane

- `producer`
- `architecture`
- `game-designer`

## Inputs

- Design change
- Affected areas
- Risk notes

## Steps

1. List the affected areas.
2. State the propagation order.
3. Mark what needs verification.
4. Hand back the change propagation note.

## Outputs

- Change
- Affected areas
- Notes

## Exit criteria

- The affected areas are all named and scheduled.

- `templates/propagate-design-change.md`
