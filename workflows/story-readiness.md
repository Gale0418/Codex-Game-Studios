# Story Readiness

## Purpose

- Check whether a story is ready for implementation.

## Trigger

- Use before moving a story into dev work.

## Primary lane

- `producer`
- `qa-regression`
- `implementation`

## Inputs

- Story
- Acceptance notes
- Dependencies

## Steps

1. Check whether the story is self-contained.
2. Note missing acceptance detail.
3. Flag dependencies that still block it.
4. Hand back the readiness call.

## Outputs

- Story
- Readiness
- Gaps

## Exit criteria

- The story is ready or explicitly blocked.

## Template

- `templates/story-readiness.md`
