# Playtest Report

## Purpose

- Summarize playtest findings and player-feel issues.

## Trigger

- Use after a playtest or targeted session.

## Primary lane

- `qa-regression`
- `game-designer`
- `docs-release`

## Inputs

- Playtest notes
- Observed behavior
- Goals

## Steps

1. Summarize the session.
2. Call out player-feel issues.
3. Note the highest-value follow-up.
4. Hand back the report.

## Outputs

- Session
- Findings
- Recommendation

## Exit criteria

- The playtest can inform the next change.

## Template

- `templates/playtest-report.md`
