# Team Narrative

## Purpose

- Focus on narrative direction, tone, and content consistency.

## Trigger

- Use when story text or narrative structure changes.

## Primary lane

- `narrative-director`
- `writer`
- `docs-release`

## Inputs

- Narrative scope
- Tone targets
- Content notes

## Steps

1. State the narrative goal.
2. Check consistency and tone.
3. Note any content gaps.
4. Hand back the narrative notes.

## Outputs

- Narrative notes
- Risks
- Verification

## Exit criteria

- The narrative direction is clear enough to write against.

## Template

- `templates/team-narrative.md`
