# Specialist Pools

## Engine pools

Use these when an engine choice is known. They are additive: pick one engine pool plus the relevant discipline pool(s).

### Engine-agnostic pool (default)

- `engine-generalist`
- `engine-programmer`
- `tools-programmer`
- `ui-programmer`
- `technical-artist`
- `build-release-engineer`

Use for engine-neutral guidance, portable architecture, and baseline tooling.
